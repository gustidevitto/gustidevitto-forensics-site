### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, Gross Profit Leakage is the difference between what you *should* have made (Theoretical GP) and what you *actually* have (Actual GP).

**The Calculation:**

**(Theoretical Gross Profit - Actual Gross Profit) / Theoretical Gross Profit**

**Forensic Classification:**
- < 3% → **The Tight Ship** (Surgically efficient)
- 3-7% → **The Normal Wear** (Acceptable baseline)
- 7-12% → **The Active Bleed** (Vulnerability detected)
- > 12% → **The Profit Drain** (Systemic failure)

**In 612 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
- 91% of businesses are in the Active Bleed or Profit Drain zone.
- **The "Invisible" Factor:** 84% of leakage happens in the "Small Data"—untracked adjustments, portioning errors, and "grace" discounts.
- **The Annual Impact:** For a business doing $1M/year, even a "minor" 10% leakage is a **$100,000 loss in net profit.** That is an entire manager's salary or a new outlet.

Losing $1 is a mistake. Losing 12.4% is a choice. **Leakage is the price of negligence.**
