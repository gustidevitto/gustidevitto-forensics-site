### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, the LTV : CAC Ratio measures the efficiency of your growth engine. It determines if your "Scale" is sustainable or destructive.

**The Calculation:**

**Lifetime Value (Profit-based) / Customer Acquisition Cost**

**Forensic Classification:**
- > 3:1 → **The Growth Engine** (Surgically efficient, ready to scale)
- 1.5 - 3.0 → **The Normal Cycle** (Viable but needs optimization)
- 1.0 - 1.5 → **The Danger Zone** (Vulnerable to any CAC spike)
- < 1.0 → **The Death Spiral** (Growth = Bankruptcy)

**In 543 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
