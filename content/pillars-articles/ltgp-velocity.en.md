### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, LTGP Velocity (Long-Term Gross Profit Velocity) measures the **acceleration of your profit engine**, not just its size.

**The Calculation:**

**(Current Month Gross Profit - Previous Month Gross Profit) / Previous Month Gross Profit**

**Forensic Classification:**
- > +5% → **Accelerating** (Healthy momentum)
- +2% to +5% → **Growing** (Stable but slowing)
- 0% to +2% → **Stalling** (The Danger Zone)
- < 0% → **Contracting** (Active structural decline)

**In 543 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
- 87% of businesses are in the Stalling or Contracting zone.
- **The Expansion Trap:** 68% of owners try to "fix" a stalling business by opening new outlets. This increases Revenue but **devastates Velocity** as the new outlets are often less efficient.
- **The Early Warning:** Velocity drops an average of **96 days** before the first "Loss" appears on a P&L. It is the ultimate leading indicator of failure.

Size is status. Velocity is strength. **LTGP Velocity is the future.**
