### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, the Net Cash Lock Period measures the total "Hold Time" of your working capital—from the moment you spend $1 to the moment it returns as $1.10.

**The Calculation:**

**Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO) + Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) − Days Payable Outstanding (DPO)**

**Forensic Classification:**
- < 15 Days → **The Cash Engine** (Surgically efficient)
- 15-30 Days → **The Normal Cycle** (Sustainable)
- 30-60 Days → **The Hostage Situation** (Active cash suppression)
- > 60 Days → **The Death Spiral** (Liquidity time bomb)

**In 412 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
- 79% of businesses are in the Hostage Situation zone.
- **The "Stagnant" Factor:** Inventory (DIO) accounts for 65% of the hostage duration in most businesses.
- **The Liquidity Surge:** Every 1-day reduction in this period typically frees up **$18k - $45k in cash per outlet.**

Inventory is a prison. AR is a delay. **The Period is the sentence.**
