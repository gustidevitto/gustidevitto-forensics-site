### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, Cash Realization Lag is the distance between the *moment of sale* and the *moment of cash availability*.

**The Calculation:**

**Date Cash Received – Date of Transaction**

**Forensic Classification:**
- < 3 Days → **The Cash Engine** (Instant liquidity)
- 3-7 Days → **The Normal Cycle** (Sustainable)
- 7-14 Days → **The Danger Zone** (Vulnerable to shocks)
- > 14 Days → **The Death Trap** (Living on "Time Debt")

**In 543 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
- 81% of businesses are in the Danger Zone or Death Trap.
- **The "Aggregator" Crisis:** Most delivery platforms withhold cash for 7-14 days. If your aggregator sales hit 40%, your business is structurally delayed by 2 weeks.
- **The Liquidity Illusion:** Owners often celebrate a "Record Sales Day" on Friday, not realizing that record sales day won't help them pay the rent on Monday.

Cash in the register is a promise. Cash in the bank is a reality. **The Lag is the risk.**
