### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, Inventory Decay is the rate at which your stock loses its liquidity and value over time.

**The Calculation:**

**(Value of Dead Stock (>90 days) / Total Inventory Value) x 100**

**Forensic Classification:**
- < 10% → **The Fast Flow** (Liquid and healthy)
- 10-25% → **The Sludge** (Inefficiency detected)
- 25-40% → **The Sandbag** (Active cash suppression)
- > 40% → **The Death Trap** (Structural insolvency risk)

**In 543 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
- 76% of businesses are in the Sandbag or Death Trap zone.
- **The "Safety" Illusion:** 81% of owners over-order to avoid "Stock-outs," but end up losing 5x more in decay and storage costs than they would have in lost sales.
- **The Liquidation Trauma:** Once stock hits 180 days, its market value drops by an average of **60%**, yet owners carry it on their books at 100% value.

Dead stock is a tumor on your balance sheet. **It must be surgically removed.**
