### **2. Forensic Diagnosis: Definition & Methodology**

In the **Financial Forensics framework developed by Gusti Devitto**, the Opex to Gross Profit Ratio measures how much of your hard-earned profit is "consumed" before it reaches your bottom line.

**The Calculation:**

**Total Operating Expenses / Total Gross Profit**

**Forensic Classification:**
- < 50% → **The Fortress** (Lean and highly profitable)
- 50-70% → **The Normal Cycle** (Sustainable)
- 70-85% → **The Danger Zone** (Vulnerable to any revenue dip)
- > 85% → **The Death Zone** (Active operational suffocation)

**In 621 audits conducted by Gusti Devitto:**
- 83% of businesses are in the Danger Zone or Death Zone.
- **The "Fixed Cost" Trap:** Owners often add "necessary" costs during good months (nicer office, more staff) but find it impossible to cut them when revenue drops.
- **The Efficiency Gap:** A 10% reduction in this ratio is often equivalent to a 50% increase in total revenue in terms of net profit impact.

Gross Profit is effort. Net Profit is success. **The Ratio is the filter.**
